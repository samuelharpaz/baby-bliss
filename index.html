Why Baby Bliss

Hi, I'm Naava, a dedicated sleep consultant passionate about helping families thrive. Through research, experience (I'm a mother of 8), and a deep understanding of baby cues and sleep patterns, I've developed a proven, gentle approach that helps babies sleep better while fostering a secure parent-child bond.

For over a decade, I've helped hundreds of families establish healthy sleep routines, reduce night waking's, and create peaceful bedtime habits—without unnecessary stress. Whether you're preparing for your baby's arrival, or looking to transform sleep struggles into restful nights, I'm here to guide you with personalized sleep plans and expert support.

Learn more about me

Proven methods

Sleep training doesn't have to be stressful—it can (and in my opinion, should) be a gentle and natural process. Utilizing my own proven methods (inspired by Dr. Harvey Karp, Tracy Hogg “The Baby Whisperer,” and Ina May Gaskin), I've helped hundreds of families create flexible routines, reduce colic, improve sleep, and build a peaceful home.

Our approach

There's no one-size-fits-all solution to baby sleep. My evidence-based methods honor your instincts while creating a personalized sleep plan that fits your family's needs. By understanding the connection between eating, playing, and sleeping, I'll help you recognize baby cues and establish gentle, healthy routines for better sleep.
