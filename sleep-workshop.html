Booking FAQ

  • Where in Los Angeles is the in-person workshop currently offered?

    The in-person workshop (additional $100) is generally available within Los Angeles zip codes 90035 and 90036. If you're located in the vicinity but outside this area, you're still welcome to request the in-person option—we'll check your location and let you know if we can make it work.

  • What sort of follow-up support can I expect after taking the workshop?

    The workshop includes one week of follow-up support (two weeks for a first baby) via text or WhatsApp, with an optional extended support upgrade. While I'm not available 24/7, I do my utmost to respond within 24 hours. Please note that support is unavailable from Friday afternoon through Saturday evening.

  • What is included in the extended support option?

    The extended support upgrades the post-workshop text/WhatsApp support from the standard 1 week to 4 weeks. For first baby or twins, support is extended from 2 weeks to 5 weeks. Check out our Premium Sleep Bundle as well for the most comprehensive support package we offer.
