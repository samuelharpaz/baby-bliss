Baby Bliss Gift Cards

We offer a beautifully designed virtual gift card which upon purchase will be sent to the recipient via email.

The process is just a few simple steps:

  1. Fill out the form below, including your own information as well as the full name and email address of the recipient.

  2. Along with the regular workshop ($350) which is typically given via phone or video call and can be taken anywhere, in-person sessions ($450) are offered in select areas in Los Angeles, California, as scheduling permits (typically within 90035 and 90036 zip codes).

    If the recipient is located within this vicinity and you would like to request in-person sessions, simply indicate this in the comments box at the bottom of the form along with the recipient's full address. We will check if it's possible to accommodate this request, and of course get back to you.

  3. We will send you a payment link to purchase the gift card. Upon payment completion, the gift card will be sent to the recipient's email as a gift in your name, along with all the information they need to set up the workshop!

For any questions or assistance, contact me at (323)840-4802 (phone, text, or WhatsApp).
