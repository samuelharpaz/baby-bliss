Purchase a Gift Card

We offer a beautifully designed virtual gift card which upon purchase will be sent to the recipient via email.

The process is quick and easy—just a few simple steps:

  1. Fill out the form below, including your own information as well as the full name and email address of the recipient.

  2. Along with the regular workshop ($350) which is typically given via phone or video call and can be taken anywhere, we also offer in-person sessions ($450) in select areas in Los Angeles, California, as scheduling permits (typically within 90035 and 90036 zip codes).

    If the recipient is located in or near this area and you would like to request in-person sessions, simply select the option in the form below, and fill out the recipient's street address and zip code in Los Angeles. We will check if it's possible to accommodate this request, and of course get back to you.

  3. We will review the information with you for accuracy. Upon payment completion, the gift card will be sent to the recipient's email as a gift in your name, along with a brief introduction to our program and all the information they need to set up the workshop!

For any questions, contact me at (323)840-4802 (phone, text, or WhatsApp) and I'll be happy to assist.
