Baby Bliss Gift Cards

Gifting a Baby Bliss gift card to a loved one is gifting peace of mind.

We offer beautifully designed virtual gift cards which upon purchase will be sent to the recipient via email.

The process is just a few simple steps:

  1. Choose between two options, the virtual workshop (over phone/video call), or the in-person workshop. Please note that the in-person option is only available in select zip codes in Los Angeles, California.
  2. Fill out the form below, including your own information as well as the full name and email address of the recipient.
  3. We will double-check that all the information is in order (if necessary, we will contact you to make sure everything is correct).
  4. We will send you a payment link to purchase the gift card. Upon payment completion, you will receive a confirmation email. The gift card will be sent to the recipient's email as a gift in your name, along with all the information needed to set up the workshop!

For any questions, please contact me at (323)840-4802.
