Our Classes

Prenatal Bonding & Sleep Foundations

Laying the Foundation for a Secure Start

The prenatal stage is the ideal time to prepare for newborn care, sleep routines, and bonding techniques that will set you and your baby up for success. This course helps parents create a secure, loving environment from day one. Understanding your baby's needs before birth allows for a smoother transition into parenthood and helps prevent sleep challenges from the start.

Select Prenatal Coaching

What you'll learn:

  • How your baby's sleep cycles develop from birth and what to expect.
  • Learn baby bonding techniques using touch, voice, and interaction.
  • Be ready to establish a healthy feeding and sleep routine from day one.
  • Set up a sleep-friendly environment for optimal rest.
  • Understand how to reduce colic and fussiness early on.
  • Navigate the roles of one or both parents in newborn care and bonding.

Newborn Sleep & Care (0-6 Months)

Supporting Your Baby's First Months

A structured approach to newborn sleep, feeding, and bonding designed for all parents, whether you're a first-time parent or have previous experience. This course provides essential skills to help your newborn feel secure, calm, and naturally develop healthy sleep patterns. Starting early sets the stage for lifelong healthy sleep habits. By creating a nurturing routine, your baby will learn to self-soothe and sleep soundly at the appropriate age.

Select Newborn Coaching

What you'll learn:

  • Create a structured daily routine.
  • Develop bonding techniques to strengthen connection.
  • Master feeding techniques to ensure your baby stays full longer.
  • Use gentle sleep strategies to encourage self-soothing.
  • How to calm a fussy baby.
  • Read your baby's cues for hunger, comfort, and sleep.

Transition Support (6-10 Months)

Helping Your Baby Transition & Thrive

Between 6-10 months, babies begin to outgrow their early routines, signaling the need for structured transitions. Whether your baby has been following my method from birth or you're just getting started, this course will help ensure a smooth shift to Stage 2 sleep and development.

Select Baby/Transition Coaching

What you'll learn:

  • Identify developmental milestones.
  • Support physical development through rolling, crawling, and playtime.
  • Wean from night feedings (if appropriate for your baby's age).
  • Techniques for self-soothing and independent sleep
  • Create an evolving sleep schedule to support growth.
  • Break unwanted sleep habits in a stress-free way.

Gift to a new Mom

Even more questions…

  • My husband cares for our baby differently than I do. Will this confuse our baby?

    Not at all! Babies thrive when bonding with both parents. While you’ll both follow the same framework I teach, each of you brings a unique style — and your baby will grow to love both. Daddy’s touch, scent, and voice will help build security, adaptability, and emotional strength.

  • Does your method work for both breastfed and bottle-fed babies?

    Yes! My approach supports both breastfeeding and bottle-feeding families, ensuring a consistent sleep and feeding routine tailored to your baby’s needs.

  • My baby was born underweight, and my doctor is still monitoring growth. Is it safe to start?​

    If your baby is underweight and being monitored weekly, I recommend waiting or consulting your doctor before starting any structured sleep or feeding plan. Your baby’s growth and health always come first!
