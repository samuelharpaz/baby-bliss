The Premium Sleep Bundle

$1000

Our all-inclusive package designed for families who want ongoing expert support through every stage of their baby's sleep journey.

It begins with our signature Baby Sleep Workshop, a personalized one-on-one session that lays the foundation for healthy sleep habits tailored to your child and your parenting style. The bundle also includes the Father's Support Session, offering dads their own dedicated space to gain clarity, ask questions, and feel more confident and involved in the process. As your baby grows, Transition Support training (our workshop for babies 5-12 months) provides expert guidance through common milestones and development stages so you can adjust smoothly and stay on track.

To ensure you feel supported every step of the way, the bundle includes 6 months of direct support, through 30-minute phone check-ins every other week to troubleshoot, recalibrate, and track your baby's progress together. In between calls, you'll have unlimited text and WhatsApp support for the entire 6 months, so you can reach out anytime with questions or concerns.
